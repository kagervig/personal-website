⚙️ Practical Tips
Payment & Money
  • Currency: Chinese Yuan (¥, CNY). 1 USD ≈ 7-8 CNY
  • WeChat Pay & Alipay: Essential. Most places accept these apps
  • ATMs: Widely available in cities; may have withdrawal limits
  • Credit cards: Less common; carry cash or use mobile payments
Communications
  • SIM cards: Buy at airport; bring passport
  • WiFi: Available at hotels, cafes, malls (often requires registration)
  • VPN: Set up before arrival for accessing blocked websites
  • Must-have apps: WeChat, Alipay, Baidu Maps, Google Translate, Didi
Getting Around
  • Metro: Fastest in major cities
  • Taxis: Use meter or Didi app; generally cheap
  • High-speed trains: Book via 12306.cn; frequent & reliable
  • Buses: Cheap but crowded; use Baidu Maps
Cultural Notes
  • Respect for elders and hierarchies is important
  • Avoid discussing politics or sensitive topics
  • Photography in temples/museums may require permission
  • Tipping is NOT customary
  • Don't point with chopsticks or leave them upright in rice (funeral symbolism)
Common Scams to Avoid
  • Overly friendly "students" wanting tea or sightseeing
  • Inflated taxi fares—always use meter or apps
  • Counterfeit tickets—buy from official sources
  • Unsolicited "guides" demanding excessive payment
